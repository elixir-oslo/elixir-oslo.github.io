"ELIXIR Oslo services" (the "Services") include all IT services maintained by the Oslo node of ELIXIR Norway that contain a reference to the [General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance Documentation for ELIXIR Oslo services](index.html) or any of its subpages, clearly marked as "Terms of service", "Privacy policy", "GDPR compliance", "Service agreement", or similar.
